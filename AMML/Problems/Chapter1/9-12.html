(ii) $|(|a+b| - |a| - |b|)|.$
(iii) $|(|a+b| + |c| - |a+b+c|)|.$
(iv) $|x^2 - 2xy + y^2|.$
(v) $|(|\sqrt{2} + \sqrt{3}| - |\sqrt{5} + \sqrt{7}|)|.$

(i) $|a + b| - |b|.$
(ii) $|(|x| - 1)|.$

(iv) $a - |(a - |a|)|.$

12. Prove the following:
(i) $|xy| = |x| \cdot |y|.$
(ii) $\lvert 1/x \ \rvert = 1/|x|, \ \text{if} \ x \neq 0. \ (\text{The best way to do this is to remember what}$ $|x|^{-1}$ is.)
(iii) $|x|/|y| = \lvert x/y \rvert, \ \text{if} \ y \neq 0.$
(iv) $|x - y| \leq |x| + |y|.$ (Give a very short proof.)
(v) $|x| - |y| \leq |x - y|.$ (A very short proof is possible if you write things in the right way.)
(vi) $|(|x| - |y|)| \leq |x - y|.$ (Why does this follow immediately from (v)?)
(vii) $|x+y+z| \leq |x| + |y| + |z|.$ Indicate when equality holds, and prove your statement.
